10-from-top Stock Screener

Self-built rebuild of the Trendlyne screener "10% from top". Stocks at or near 52-week highs after at least doubling from their lows, with growing profits.

Criterion 1% distance from 52-week LOW > 100
Criterion 2% distance from 52-week HIGH < 10
Criterion 3Latest-quarter Net Profit YoY > 0
Universe: Nifty 500 Data source: Yahoo Finance
